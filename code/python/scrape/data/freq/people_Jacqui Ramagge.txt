She was the Head of School of Mathematics and Applied Statistics from 2009\u20132015.[5]\n Ramagge became a professor at the University of Sydney in 2015 and was the Head of School of Mathematics and Statistics from 2015 to 2019.[5] During this time, she supported the establishment of the Sydney Mathematical Research Institute.[8]\n Ramagge returned to the United Kingdom to take up a leadership position at Durham University as Executive Dean (Science).[2]\n Ramagge\u2019s research interests overlap algebra, analysis and geometry.
