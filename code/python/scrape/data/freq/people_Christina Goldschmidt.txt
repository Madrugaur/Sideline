Norris.[3]\n She did postdoctoral research with Jean Bertoin at Pierre and Marie Curie University, as a Stokes Fellow at Pembroke College, Cambridge, and as an EPSRC Postdoctoral Fellow at Oxford, before becoming an assistant professor in 2009 at the University of Warwick.
