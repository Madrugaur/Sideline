Binnig, Quate and Gerber were rewarded with the Kavli-Prize in 2016 for developing the Atomic Force Microscope.\n Quate was a member of the National Academy of Engineering and National Academy of Sciences. Liebmann Memorial Award and the IEEE Medal of Honor in 1988 for \"the invention and development of the scanning acoustic microscope.\"[4] Quate became a senior research fellow at the Palo Alto Research Center (PARC) in 1984.[4] In 2000, he became a recipient of the Joseph F. Keithley Award For Advances in Measurement Science.
