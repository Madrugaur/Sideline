Platt Chaired Professor for Teaching Excellence in 2014,[2][4] while still an associate professor. She was promoted to full professor in 2017.[2]\n Martonosi has also been active in the leadership of INFORMS, including terms as chair of the Academic Programs Database Committee and as president of the Forum on Women in OR/MS.[4]\n In 2012, Martonosi won the Henry L.
