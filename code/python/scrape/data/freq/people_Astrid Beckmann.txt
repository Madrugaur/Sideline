She completed her postdoctorate at this latter institution in 2003 in mathematics education. Beckmann\u2019s thesis addressed the development of a model concept for interdisciplinary teaching, with a focus on mathematics in connection to physics, language and computer science.[10]\n Following a brief period as an Associate Professor at Leibniz University Hannover,[11] Beckmann was appointed Professor of Mathematics and Mathematics Education at the University of Education Schw\u00e4bisch Gm\u00fcnd[12][13] in 2003. Beckmann was deputy chair of the State Rectors\u2019 Conference for Universities of Education (LRK[14]) from 2011 to 2015, subsequently taking over as chair between April 2015 and 2017.
