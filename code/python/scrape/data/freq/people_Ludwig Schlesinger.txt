In 1933 he was forced to retire by the Nazis. He died shortly afterwards.[2] His doctoral students include Abraham Plessner.\n Schlesinger was a historian of science. He wrote an article on the function theory of Carl Friedrich Gauss and translated Ren\u00e9 Descartes' La G\u00e9om\u00e9trie into German (1894).
