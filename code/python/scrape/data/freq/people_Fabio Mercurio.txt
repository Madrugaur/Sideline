With Damiano Brigo (2002\u20132003), he has shown how to construct stochastic differential equations consistent with mixture models, applying this to volatility smile modeling in the context of local volatility models.[1] He is also one of the main authors in inflation modeling.[2] Mercurio has also authored several publications in top journals and co-authored the book Interest rate models: theory and practice for Springer-Verlag,[3] that quickly became an international reference for stochastic dynamic interest rate modeling.
