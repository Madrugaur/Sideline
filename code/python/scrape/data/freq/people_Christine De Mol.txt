She is a professor of mathematics at the Universit\u00e9 libre de Bruxelles, and the former chair of the SIAM Activity Group on Imaging Science.\n De Mol was educated at the Universit\u00e9 libre de Bruxelles, earning a licence in physics in 1975 and a Ph.D. in 1979, with a dissertation Sur la r\u00e9gularisation des probl\u00e8mes inverses lin\u00e9aires[1]\nunder the joint supervision of Jean Reignier and Mario Bertero.[2]\n De Mol became a researcher for the Belgian National Fund for Scientific Research (FNRS), obtaining a permanent position there in 1981 and becoming a director of research in 1996. In 1998 she gave up her position with the FNRS, becoming an honorary researcher with them, to become a full professor at the Universit\u00e9 libre de Bruxelles.
