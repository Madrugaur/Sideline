Her research interests include number theory and modular forms.[2]\n Choie graduated from Ewha Womans University in 1982,[2] and earned a doctorate in 1986 from Temple University under the supervision of Marvin Knopp.[3] After temporary positions at Ohio State University and the University of Maryland, she became an assistant professor at University of Colorado in 1989, and moved to POSTECH as a full professor in 1990.
