An example of Derby Gothic by John Holloway Sanders\nJohn Holloway Sanders FRIBA (1825 \u2013 16 October 1884) was an architect based in England and chief architect of the Midland Railway until 1884.\nHis date of appointment as Chief Architect to the Midland Railway is not known, but he is recorded as working for them in 1845.
