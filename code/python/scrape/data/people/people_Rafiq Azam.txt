Rafiq Azam\u09b0\u09ab\u09bf\u0995 \u0986\u099c\u09aeAzam in 2011Born (1963-12-29) December 29, 1963 (age\u00a057)NationalityBangladeshiAlma\u00a0materBangladesh University of Engineering and TechnologyOccupationArchitect\nMuhammad Rafiq Azam (born 29 December 1963)[1] is a Bangladeshi architect who is principal architect at Shatotto Architecture. \n\nContents\n\n1 Career\n2 Projects\n3 Awards\n4 Invited Speaker\n5 Exhibitions\n6 References\n7 External links\n\n\nCareer[edit]\nAzam graduated from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology in 1989.[1]\nIn 2016, Dhaka South City Corporation launched the \"Jol Sobujer Dhaka Project\" with a 2 billion Bangladeshi Taka ($24M as of 2016) budget to revitalize 19 parks and 12 playgrounds.[2][3] As part of the project, Azam and his team at Shatotto Architecture redesigned the 1.3-acre (0.53\u00a0ha) Shahid Abdul Alim Playground in Old Dhaka. In January 2017, Azam and his team began work on the renovation of 29-acre (12\u00a0ha) Osmani Udyan park in Dhaka.
