She was elected to the Executive Committee of AWM and served on that committee from 2016-2020. In addition, she chaired the AWM Awards Committee from 2016-2018.[8]  Woodward was selected as a Fellow of the Association for Women in Mathematics in the Class of 2021 \"for her sustained commitment
