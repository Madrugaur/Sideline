She has been the Moses Professor at NYU since 1999.[3][4][5]\n Young became a Sloan Fellow in 1985,[3][5] and a Guggenheim Fellow in 1997.[4][7]\n In 1993, Young was given the Ruth Lyttle Satter Prize
