In her role at BAE Systems Inc., Hudson is the first woman to be the head of a company that is a major contractor with the Pentagon.[6] She currently serves on the board of directors for Bank of America[7] and Ingersoll Rand.[8]\n\nUniversity diplomas[edit]\nA graduate of the University of Florida,[9] Hudson received her bachelor's degree with honors in Industrial and Systems Engineering in 1972.
