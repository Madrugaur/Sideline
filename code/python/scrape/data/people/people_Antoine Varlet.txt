However, he quickly turned from 1929 onwards to an Art Deco style mixed with elements of Beaux-Arts style. As he died young and did not participate after the war in the blossoming of apartment buildings, his work, which marks the Brussels landscape, has not yet been the subject of an in-depth study.\n His specialty was makings buildings at street corners which give a wider perspective.
