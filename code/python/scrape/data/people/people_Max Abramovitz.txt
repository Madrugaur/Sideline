He graduated in 1929 from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign School of Architecture. While a Junior at Illinois, Abramovitz was a member of the Tau Epsilon Phi fraternity.[1]  He later received an M.S. from Columbia University's architecture school in 1931.
