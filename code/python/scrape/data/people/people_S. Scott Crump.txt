He is currently the chairman of the board of directors of Stratasys, which produces additive manufacturing machines for direct digital manufacturing (a.k.a. rapid manufacturing); these machines are popularly called \u201c3D printers.\u201d He took the manufacturing company public in 1994 (Nasdaq:SSYS).
