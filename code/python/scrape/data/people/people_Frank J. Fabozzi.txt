Fabozzi is an American economist, educator, writer, and investor, currently Professor of Finance at EDHEC Business School and a Member of Edhec Risk Institute.[1] He was previously a Professor in the Practice of Finance and Becton Fellow in the Yale School of Management. He has authored and edited many acclaimed books, three of which were coauthored with Nobel laureates, Franco Modigliani and Harry Markowitz.
