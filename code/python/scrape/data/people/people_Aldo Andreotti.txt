Notably he proved the Andreotti\u2013Frankel theorem, the Andreotti\u2013Grauert theorem, the Andreotti\u2013Vesentini theorem and introduced, jointly with Fran\u00e7ois Norguet, the Andreotti\u2013Norguet integral representation for functions of several complex variables.\nAndreotti was a visiting scholar at the Institute for Advanced Study in 1951 and again from 1957 through 1959.[1]\n\nContents\n\n1 Selected publications\n\n1.1 Articles\n1.2 Books\n\n\n2 See also\n3 Notes\n4 References\n\n4.1 Biographical and general references\n4.2 Scientific references\n\n\n5 External links\n\n\nSelected publications[edit]\nAldo Andreotti published 100 scientific works, including papers, books and lecture notes: many of them, except all his books but (Andreotti & Nacinovich 1980), are collected in his \"Selecta\" (Andreotti\u00a01982, 1992, 1994, 1999). In his \"Selecta\" are also included three unpublished sets of lecture notes,[2] the first one prepared by Philippe Artzner from a course on the theory of analytic functions of several complex variables held by Andreotti during winter 1961 at the University of Strasbourg,[3] the second and third ones taken from two lectures held by Francesco Gherardelli at the \"Seminario di Geometria\"[4] of the Scuola Normale Superiore during the years 1971\u20131972 and 1971\u20131972 respectively, on topics concerning his joint work with Andreotti:[5] despite their nature of unpublished works, Vesentini (1992, p.\u00a05) states that they have brought significant contributions to research.[6]\n\nArticles[edit]\nAndreotti, Aldo; Grauert, Hans (1962), \"Th\u00e9or\u00e8mes de finitude pour la cohomologie des espaces complexes\" [Finiteness theorems for the cohomology of complex spaces], Bulletin de la Soci\u00e9t\u00e9 Math\u00e9matique de France (in French), 90: 193\u2013259, MR\u00a00150342, Zbl\u00a00106.05501, archived from the original on 2014-08-06, retrieved 2012-08-04.\nAndreotti, Aldo; Vesentini, Edoardo (1965), \"Carleman estimates for the Laplace-Beltrami equation on complex manifolds\", Publications Math\u00e9matiques de l'IH\u00c9S, 25: 81\u2013130, doi:10.1007/bf02684398, ISSN\u00a01618-1913, MR\u00a00175148, Zbl\u00a00138.06604.\nAndreotti, Aldo; Norguet, Fran\u00e7ois (20 January 1964), \"Probl\u00e8me de Levi pour les classes de cohomologie\" [The Levi problem for cohomology classes], Comptes rendus hebdomadaires des s\u00e9ances de l'Acad\u00e9mie des Sciences (in French), 258 (Premi\u00e8re partie): 778\u2013781, MR\u00a00159960, Zbl\u00a00124.38803.\nAndreotti, Aldo; Vesentini, Edoardo (1965b), \"Erratum.
