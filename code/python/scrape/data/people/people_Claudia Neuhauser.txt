She has also held faculty positions at the University of Southern California, University of Wisconsin\u2013Madison, and University of California, Davis[5]\n Before moving to the University of Minnesota Rochester in 2008, she was Professor and Head of the Ecology, Evolution and Behavior department at the Twin Cities campus.
