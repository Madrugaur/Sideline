Her dissertation, Stochastic Differential Equations In A Hilbert Space, was supervised by Peter Falb.[2][4]\n She then joined the faculty at Purdue University, but in 1971 moved to the University of Warwick. Her dissertation, Stochastic Differential Equations In A Hilbert Space, was supervised by Peter Falb.[2][4]\nShe then joined the faculty at Purdue University, but in 1971 moved to the University of Warwick. In 1977 she moved again, to the University of Groningen, where she remained until her 2006 retirement.[2]\n\nBooks[edit]\nCurtain was the author of:\n\nFunctional Analysis in Modern Applied Mathematics (with A.
