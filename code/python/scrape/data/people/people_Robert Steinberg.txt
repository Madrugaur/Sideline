Steinberg joined the Mathematics Department at UCLA the same year. He retired from UCLA in 1992.\n\nAwards[edit]\nSteinberg was an invited speaker at the International Congress of Mathematicians in 1966, won the Steele Prize in 1985, was elected to the United States National Academy of Sciences in 1985, and won the Jeffery\u2013Williams Prize in 1990.
