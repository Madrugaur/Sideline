Her mother, Zofia Szmydtowa (n\u00e9e G\u0105siorowska), was a historian and philologist.[1]\n Szmydt studied at the University of Warsaw in clandestine classes during the Second World War. She defended her doctoral thesis in 1949, written under the direction of Tadeusz Wa\u017cewski.[2]\n Szmydt died on 27 November 2010.[3]\n Until 1952, Szmydt worked at the Jagiellonian University.
