universality for invariant matrix ensembles.[5]\nIn the spectral theory of random Schr\u00f6dinger operators, he introduced the class of metrically transitive operators, and discovered several fundamental properties of this class.[6] Together with Ilya Goldsheid and Stanislav Molchanov, he established Anderson localization for a class of one-dimensional self-adjoint operators with random potentials;[7] this was the first mathematically rigorous proof of Anderson localization.[8]\nAwards and honors[edit]\nHe is a member of the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine.[9]  In 2012 he
