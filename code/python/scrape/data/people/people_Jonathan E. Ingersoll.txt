Israel Professor of International Trade and Finance at Yale School of Management. Prior to coming to Yale he was on the faculty at the Graduate School of Business at the University of Chicago.\nIngersoll's research area is finance, focusing on asset valuation\u2014the pricing of options and futures and the term structure of interest rates. He is one of the authors of the Cox\u2013Ingersoll\u2013Ross model of the yield curve.\nIngersoll is a founding member of the Society for Financial Studies and has held editorial positions at several journals, including the Review of Financial Studies and the Journal of Finance.
