She went to Syracuse University for graduate study in mathematics, earning a master's degree in 1970 and completing her doctorate in 1974.[3]\nHer dissertation, On the Degree of Approximation of a Function by Partial Sums of its Fourier Series, concerned approximation theory, and was supervised by Daniel Waterman.[4]\n At the University of Utah, Cohen became the first woman to gain tenure at the School of Engineering.[5]\n With Richard F.
