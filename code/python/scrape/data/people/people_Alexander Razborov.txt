In particular, Razborov and Rudich showed that, under the assumption that certain kinds of one-way functions exist, such proofs cannot give a resolution of the P = NP problem, so new techniques will be required in order to solve this question.\n\nAwards[edit]\nNevanlinna Prize (1990) for introducing the \"approximation method\" in proving Boolean circuit lower bounds of some essential algorithmic problems,[1]\nErd\u0151s Lecturer, Hebrew University of Jerusalem, 1998.\nCorresponding member of the Russian Academy of Sciences (2000)[2][3]\nDavid P.
