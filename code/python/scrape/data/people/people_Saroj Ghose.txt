Parliament Museum, New Delhi[2]\nRashtrapati Bhavan Museum, New Delhi, IndiaTitlePhDAwardsPadma Shri, Padma Bhushan\nSaroj Ghose (born 1   September 1935 in Kolkata) is an Indian science popularizer and museum maker. He was the director of Birla Industrial & Technological Museum and director general of the National Council of Science Museums, Government of India. He was also the President of the International Council of Museums in Paris during 1992-98. He won many awards including one for \"Best Effort in Science Popularisation Amongst Children\". He has also mentored some of India's best Museum Developers.\n[3]\n\nContents\n\n1 Education\n2 Awards and distinctions\n3 Advisor\n4 References\n\n\nEducation[edit]\nGhose graduated in Electrical Communication Engineering at Jadavpur University, Kolkata.
