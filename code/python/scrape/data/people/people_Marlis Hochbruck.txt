She is a professor in the Institute for Applied and Numerical Mathematics at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology.[2]\n Hochbruck went to high school in Krefeld, and studied Technomathematics at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology from 1983 to 1989. She obtained her first professorship in 1998, in applied mathematics at the University of D\u00fcsseldorf, declining two offers of professorships at other German universities in the same year.
