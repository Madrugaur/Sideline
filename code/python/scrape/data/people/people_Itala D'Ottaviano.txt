She earned a master's degree in mathematics at the University of Campinas in 1974, and completed a Ph.D. there in 1982, advised by M\u00e1rio Tourasse Teixeira and Newton da Costa, respectively.[3] Her doctoral dissertation, Sobre Uma Teoria de Modelos Trivalente, concerned the model theory of three-valued logic.[4] She earned a habilitation at the University of Campinas in 1987.[3]\n D'Ottaviano was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of California, Stanford University, and the University of Oxford.
