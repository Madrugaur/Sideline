Smith,[6]\nand based in part on her work as a visiting student at the University of Manchester, where Stafford had moved.[7]\n Walton did postdoctoral research at the University of Washington and the Mathematical Sciences Research Institute, and became a C. L. E. Moore instructor at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology from 2012 to 2015.[7]\nShe came to Temple University as Selma Lee Bloch Brown Assistant Professor of Mathematics in 2015 [1].
