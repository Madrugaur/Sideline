In 1956 he became an instructor and from 1965 a full professor at Yale, where he was chair of the mathematics department from 1974 to 1977.\nFor the academic year 1958/59 he was a Fulbright Lecturer at the University of M\u00fcnster. His doctoral students include James E. Humphreys and Daniel K.
