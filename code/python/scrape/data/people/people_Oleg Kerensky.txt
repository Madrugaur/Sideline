He was president of the Institution of Structural Engineers in 1970\u201371 and won their Gold Medal in 1977. After his death in London, the same institution began their Kerensky Memorial Conferences beginning in 1988.\n He was made a C.B.E. in 1964 and was elected a Fellow of the Royal Society in 1970.[1]\n Kerensky was the father and namesake of dance critic Oleg Kerensky, Jr (1930\u20131993).
